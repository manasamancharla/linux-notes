## The Modes
- **Command Mode** (default) – Used for navigation and command execution.
- **Insert Mode** – Used for text editing (press `i` to enter, `Esc` to exit).
- **Last Line Mode** – Used for saving, quitting, and searching (press `:` in Command mode).
